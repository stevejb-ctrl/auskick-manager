What's distinctive at U11:

  rotations work harder.

  the coach.
- Stats now have visible weight — kids notice their own goal
  count, parents start tallying.
- Bench rotation is still everyone's concern but coaches have
  more legitimate reasons to keep someone on (e.g. specific
  matchup, kid coming back from injury).
