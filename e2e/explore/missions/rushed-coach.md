break and you're rattled — your team's two goals down, parents are
asking questions, and your phone signal is patchy. You're using the
runner link your assistant shared with you (the URL you're starting
on).

You need to:

1. Reconcile the Q2 score with the other team's coach. They say it's
   3.4 vs 2.1 in their favour. Check what your phone says, fix any
   discrepancies in the per-quarter breakdown.
2. Look at your rotation for Q3. Can you tell at a glance which
   players have had the LEAST court time? Adjust the suggested
   lineup if needed.
3. Lend a player to the opposition (their team is short) — pick
   someone who's had the most court time so far.
4. Start Q3.

While doing this, **report what would have stressed you out in real
time**:

- Anywhere a tap had a slow, ambiguous, or no visible response?
- Anywhere you scrolled because you couldn't find the thing you
  needed in the obvious place?
- Anywhere two buttons did similar things and you weren't sure which?
- Anywhere a confirmation modal felt unnecessary, or anywhere you
  WISHED there was one?
- Anywhere the score recap or fairness numbers seemed to disagree
  with what you remembered?
