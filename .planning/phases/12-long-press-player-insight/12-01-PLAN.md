coach long-presses a player, the existing long-press action sheet now also
shows a trustworthy read on that kid's time — where they've played THIS game

   EXISTING `LockModal` (no new modal — reuse-before-fork).

and the VM builder are SHARED chrome — both must be consumed verbatim by the
other two sports, so design their props sport-neutrally now.
