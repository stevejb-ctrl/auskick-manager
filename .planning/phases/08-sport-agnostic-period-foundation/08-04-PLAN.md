<objective>
Make the fairness engine's season full-period threshold sport-agnostic and
per-game accurate, and add the 2-period rugby-league e2e that proves period
BOUNDARY semantics hold for a halves sport.

Replace the hardcoded `FULL_QUARTER_MS = 12 * 60 * 1000` in `suggestStartingLineup`
(fairness.ts:601, used at :607) with a TRAILING OPTIONAL param
`fullPeriodMs: number = 12 * 60 * 1000` added at the END of the signature, after
`chipModeByKey` (D-02/D-04). The back-compat default keeps the ~20 existing unit
callers green UNCHANGED; the three production callers pass the PER-GAME EFFECTIVE
quarter ms (D-03). Then extend `rugby-league-full-game-playthrough.spec.ts` to
assert the H1 boundary is a between-periods break (NOT full time) and H2 is full
time (D-10).

tracks the actual per-game clock (including per-game / per-team overrides for
finals or short games) rather than a fixed 12 min. The e2e exercises the league
(periodCount=2) path through the shared page.tsx booleans that plan 03 made
sport-agnostic.

Scope guard (D-04a): Do NOT touch `suggestSwaps` (fairness.ts:839) — its
cumulative-minutes ranking is Phase 10 / B4.

Output: fairness param + 3 production call sites + 2 mount sites threading the
effective ms + the extended rugby-league e2e.
